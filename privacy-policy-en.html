2. How We Use Information

  • Displaying ads and measuring ad performance (Google AdMob)

3. Third-Party Sharing

The Developer does not directly share personal information with third parties. However, the third-party services listed above (Google) process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage

Game progress data (upgrade levels, high scores, purchase status) is stored only on the device locally and is not transmitted to any external server.

5. Advertising & Personalized Ads

The App displays rewarded ads through Google AdMob. Users can opt out of personalized advertising through their device settings.

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Additionally, purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app product will permanently disable all advertisements.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that information has been collected from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

  • Uninstalling the App deletes all locally stored game data
